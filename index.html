Our Story

"Every dish is a tribute to the lessons she taught me."

My passion for cooking began in my Aunt Kelly's kitchen. Growing up, I spent countless Sundays at her home surrounded by family, laughter, and incredible Southern cooking. Watching her cook taught me more than recipes. It taught me the importance of hospitality, tradition, and feeding people with love.

Aunt Kelly's influence became the foundation of The Soul Spot and the inspiration behind our soul food offerings. The Caribbean side of our story comes from my husband, Richi, whose Jamaican roots bring bold island flavors and traditions to our menu.

Together, we've blended Southern comfort and Caribbean culture into one table. Every meal is prepared with love, and every guest is treated like family. Dedicated to Aunt Kelly, whose kitchen started it all.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

The Soul Spot ("we," "us") operates this website to share our menu and take catering inquiries. We keep things simple, and that includes how we handle your information.

What we collect

The only personal information we collect is what you choose to send us through the catering inquiry form: your name, your email or phone number, and the details of your event. We do not collect anything else, and we do not use cookies, advertising trackers, or analytics tools on this site.

How we use it

Inquiry information is used for one purpose: responding to you about your event. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone for marketing.

Who processes it

Form submissions are processed by Netlify, the service that hosts this website, and delivered to our email. Netlify may keep standard technical logs (such as IP addresses) as part of operating its hosting service. Fonts on this site are served by Google Fonts, and images and video are served by GitHub, which means your browser requests files from those services when the page loads. Each of these providers has its own privacy policy governing that technical data.

How long we keep it

We keep inquiry details as long as needed to handle your event and our business records, and we delete them on request.

Your choices

You can ask us at any time to see, correct, or delete the information you sent us. Email TheSoulSpot919@gmail.com and we will take care of it.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the effective date above.
